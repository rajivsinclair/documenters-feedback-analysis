Identified Clusters

Cluster 0: Logistical & Technical Challenges (724 entries - 14.7%)

This cluster focuses on external factors that impede the documenter's ability to complete assignments, including environmental constraints, time limitations, and pre-arrival information gaps.

Key Insight: Environmental constraints like keycard elevators, parking issues, connectivity problems, and unclear time commitments create significant barriers to successful documentation.
"There were keycard issues getting to the elevator..." | "The meeting length was much longer than expected..." | "WiFi connectivity was poor in the building..."

Cluster 1: Meeting Accessibility & Process (1,519 entries - 30.8%)

This cluster addresses practical barriers to participation and procedural problems within meetings, focusing on the immediate experience of attending or observing meetings and their smooth functioning.

Key Insight: Barriers to access (physical/virtual), missing agendas, audio problems, and unclear procedures significantly impact the documentation experience.
"Meeting started 45 minutes late..." | "Could not access the agenda beforehand..." | "Audio quality made it difficult to identify speakers..."

Cluster 2: Government Transparency & Accessibility (1,456 entries - 29.6%)

This cluster emphasizes the accessibility and openness of government processes to the public, highlighting barriers encountered and lack of clear procedures that hinder public understanding.

Key Insight: Difficulties in finding information, understanding procedures, and concerns about fairness highlight the need for better civic engagement resources.
"Information was difficult to find on the website..." | "The process seemed unclear to the public..." | "Meeting procedures need better explanation for citizens..."

Cluster 3: Meeting Logistics & Tech Issues (1,227 entries - 24.9%)

This cluster focuses on logistical and technical aspects of meeting delivery and accessibility, critiquing the mechanics of how meetings were experienced both in-person and virtually.

Key Insight: Audio quality problems, livestream issues, and lack of pre-meeting information significantly impact both documenters and public participation.
"Audio had significant echoing issues..." | "Livestream ended abruptly..." | "Speaker list should be provided before the meeting..."

Key Insights & Recommendations

The 4 distinct clusters reveal specific areas for targeted improvements:

  • Logistics Guide: Create comprehensive location-specific guides with parking, access, and tech requirements
  • Meeting Standards: Implement standardized checklist for facilitators including agenda distribution and audio setup
  • Civic Engagement: Develop user-friendly guides with clear instructions for public participation
  • Technical Infrastructure: Ensure reliable audio-visual equipment and stable livestream connectivity

Impact: These balanced clusters (14.7% to 30.8% each) show that documenter challenges are diverse but addressable through targeted solutions for each specific problem area.

Our Methodology

A step-by-step approach to transforming unstructured text into meaningful insights

1
Data Collection & Preparation

Extract feedback from database, clean and filter entries, removing very short responses and preparing for analysis.

2
Generate Embeddings

Use Google's Gemini embedding model to convert each feedback text into a high-dimensional numerical vector representation.

3
Dimensionality Reduction

Apply UMAP algorithm to reduce embedding dimensions while preserving relationships between similar feedback.

4
Clustering

Use HDBSCAN algorithm to automatically group similar feedback together into meaningful clusters.

5
AI Analysis

Leverage Gemini LLM to analyze sample feedback from each cluster and generate human-readable descriptions of themes.

6
Visualization & Insights

Create interactive visualizations and extract actionable insights for program improvement.

Technology Stack
Python 3.8+ Google Gemini API PostgreSQL UMAP HDBSCAN Plotly Pandas NumPy scikit-learn

Key Insights & Recommendations

Universal Challenges Identified

The clustering analysis reveals that documenters across all programs face similar challenges. Based on manual review of the feedback, common themes include:

  • Audio quality and recording difficulties
  • Meeting logistics and last-minute changes
  • Need for contextual information about meetings
  • Technical challenges with platforms and tools
  • Accessibility and venue navigation issues

Recommended Actions

Given the uniformity of experiences, implement system-wide improvements:

  • Standardized Training: Create comprehensive training applicable to all programs
  • Universal Toolkit: Develop standard equipment and software recommendations
  • Centralized Support: Build a shared knowledge base and support system
  • Cross-Program Learning: Facilitate sharing of best practices between regions
